Cell identification in high-resolution Spatial Transcriptomics (**Cellist**), computational method to perform cell segmentation on high-resolution spatial transcriptomics (ST) data, including sequncing-based (e.g. Stereo-seq and Seq-Scope) and imaging-based (e.g. seqFISH+ and STARmap) technologies.
